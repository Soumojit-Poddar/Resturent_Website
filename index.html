About Us

Welcome to FlavoursOfIndia, a vibrant destination where food, ambiance, and affordability come together to create an unforgettable dining experience. Nestled in the heart of Kolkata, we take pride in serving a rich selection of Indian and international cuisines, prepared with love and the finest ingredients.

At FlavoursOfIndia we offer:

  • Best Quality Food
  • Affordable Delights
  • Cool Ambience
  • Fully Air-Conditioned Comfort
  • A Diverse Menu

Whether you're here for a casual meal, a family gathering, or a special celebration, FlavoursOfIndia promises a delightful experience where flavor meets comfort, all at the right price. Come and discover why we're the talk of the town!
